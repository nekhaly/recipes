Ingredients:

- 2 tsp chilli powder
- 1 tsp smoked paprika powder
- 2 skinless and boneless chicken breast fillets cut into strips ( 150 g )
- 1 tbsp Flora Cuisine or 15g Flora Buttery
- 1 onion , thinly sliced
- 1 avocado
- 1 lime
- 4 flour tortillas
- 1 red pepper , sliced
- 150 g (6oz) natural low fat yogurt
- handful of chopped fresh coriander

Directions:

1. Combine the chilli, and paprika and sprinkle evenly over the chicken.
2. Heat the Flora in a non-stick frying pan, and fry the chicken with the onion until it is cooked through.
3. Halve the avocado, remove the stone and skin, and cut into small dice. Sprinkle over the lime juice.
4. To serve, divide the chicken and onion evenly between the tortillas, add the pepper, avocado, yogurt and chopped coriander, roll up and enjoy!
